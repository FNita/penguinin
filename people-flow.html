Workforce Tracking

People Flow tracking includes various applications meant to improve work operations and make workplaces safer and more secure. It's an all-encompassing solution for better efficiency and confidence at work.

People Flow Methods

The implementation of people flow relies on wireless, wearable tags that transmit signals to specific anchors, which then translate these signals into geolocation information.

The Penguin's approach to people locating focuses on the following elements:

  • Accuracy
  • Ease of use
  • Solution-oriented
  • Affordability

Considering this, our solution is centered on the utilization of BLE 5.1, the most recent version in the BLE series.

Title for the silent video illustrating the technology's operation

Why BLE 5.1?

Accuracy is recognized as the primary distinguishing factor among various technologies, and BLE 5.1 unquestionably stands at the top in terms of precision. It offers sub-meter accuracy while consuming minimal power, incurring lower costs, and imposing no additional burden on IT infrastructure. Furthermore, its exceptional compatibility extends to all output devices, including mobile phones.

Related article: BLE 5.1 vs UWB

How it works?

Thanks to BLE 5.1, tags emit CTE signals, which are subsequently captured by the anchors. These signals are then sampled into IQ values, which are reported to the positioning engine. PenguinIN's cutting-edge algorithms are then employed to precisely determine the location of objects.
