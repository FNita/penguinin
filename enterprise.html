Penguin Location Services offers solutions designed for expansive business venues, spanning from indoor and outdoor navigation to innovative IoT applications that enhance workflows and visitor experiences.

Indoor Navigation | Enterprise Experience

Implement indoor and outdoor navigation solutions to help employees and visitors efficiently navigate large corporate campuses, business parks, and expansive venues, improving productivity for employees and enhancing the overall experience for visitors.

Personalize navigation experiences, offering directions based on user preferences or specific needs within the vast corporate campus, business park, or venue.

Provide navigation solutions for employees and visitors to find available parking spaces within the expansive campus or venue, reducing congestion and improving traffic flow.

Implement navigation to control and manage access to restricted areas within the facility, ensuring that only authorized personnel can navigate to specific locations.

Learn more

Location Based Messagin

Use location-based messaging to offer discounts and special deals to visitors and employees near specific zones or retail areas.

Send immediate safety instructions to occupants based on their location to enhance safety during critical situations.

Create interactive tours with location-based messaging for informative experiences indoors and outdoors.

Send reminders for important tasks, appointments, and meetings to ensure timely attendance and organization.

Learn more

Automated Attendance

Implement automated attendance systems to streamline workforce management, making it easier for HR and management to track employee attendance and work hours across the expansive facility.

Offer automated attendance check-in for visitors and contractors entering the corporate campus, simplifying the process and enhancing security.

Geofencing for Remote Workers: Utilize geofencing technology to track attendance and work hours of employees working remotely within the corporate campus or expansive venue.
