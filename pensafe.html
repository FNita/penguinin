Infant Protection

The Infant Protection system employs a secure ankle bracelet with tear-detection, alerting if compromised. It stays in place once attached, and by defining authorized zones, triggers an immediate alert if the infant is taken beyond these boundaries. This approach prevents potential abductions and allows real-time location tracking for staff and parents.

Solutions for Helathcare Learn more

Wander Prevention

The Wander Prevention Solution is designed to address safety concerns, particularly in environments where individuals may be prone to wandering or straying beyond designated areas. It utilizes Real-Time Location System (RTLS) technology to provide constant monitoring, instantly alerting caregivers or relevant personnel when predefined boundaries are breached.

Learn more

Hand Hygiene Compliance

The Hand Hygiene Compliance system employs a technical framework to reinforce infection control measures. Leveraging sensors strategically placed in critical areas, the system monitors hand hygiene practices by capturing data on dispenser usage. Conditional access mechanisms, integrated with the system, ensure that individuals must adhere to hygiene protocols before accessing certain areas.

Learn more

Safety in Education

The PenSafe technology is tailored for emergencies in educational facilities like active shooter scenarios for Higher Education as well as K-12. Utilizing locators can transform the way security staff response to emergency situations. Giving administrators a clearer view of current events instantly for rapid response.
