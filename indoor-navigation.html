Indoor Navigation Features

Turn-by-turn Voice Guidance

Penguin's Indoor Navigation solution provides turn-by-turn blue-dot journeys with voice commands, aligning with our vision to simplify indoor positioning applications and offering a suitable solution for individuals with mobility limitations.

Floor-plan mapping

Among our array of services, we offer mapping solutions designed to create comprehensive floor plans of venues, enabling both indoor- outdoor and desk-level navigation.

Smooth transition from outdoor to indoor

Penguin Provides a continuous blue-dot navigation experience that smoothly transitions from outdoor to indoor, ensuring the blue dot updates in real-time as you progress along the route, allowing you to confirm you're heading in the correct direction.

How it works?

The BLE tag device transmits data, which is subsequently captured by strategically placed anchors within a grid of coverage points. These signals are then analyzed by our state-of-the-art algorithms to accurately determine the user's real-time location with sub-meter precision, offering turn-by-turn guidance.

BLE is a highly compatible solution, allowing seamless integration with smartphones to centralize the solution. This enables real-time location tracking and user convenience.

Indoor Navigation

Penguin's Indoor Navigation solution leverages Bluetooth low-energy (BLE) beacon infrastructure in conjunction with patent- pending particle filters to deliver precise and reliable location accuracy.

Why BLE?

While our particle filters can utilize alternative technologies such as WiFi and ultra-wideband, we have identified BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) as the most suitable technology for indoor navigation.

This choice is underpinned by its exceptional attributes. Unlike other options, BLE offers superior compatibility, prolonged battery life for supported hardware, and cost-effectiveness.

Location-Based Messaging

The location-based messaging solution encompasses various use-cases, and the aim of this implementation is to equip facilities with the capability to send notifications according to the whereabouts of personnel within the facility.

The Process of Location-Based Messaging

By harnessing both IoT and LoT technologies, the geolocation of individuals as they approach specific points of interest can trigger alerts or push notifications, offering valuable utility across various use-cases.

This functionality extends across multiple sectors. It caters to healthcare facilities seeking safety measures for restricted areas and aids retail owners in promoting offers to shoppers navigating through malls. The potential applications are numerous.

How?

By leveraging BLE technology and deploying beacons within the facility, we can pinpoint navigating users location when near a point of interest. This enables us to determine the precise locations and times for triggering specific messages.
