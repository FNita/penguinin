Infant Protection

Elevating Neonatal Intensive Care Units operations and protecting the newborns.

Request a Demo Download brochure

Stress free operations with Penguin's infant protection ankle bracelets

The Penguin infant protection solution employs a comfortable ankle bracelet that triggers an alert whenever an infant is moved without authorization. This solution is ideal for preventing abductions and ensures the elimination of any operational errors.

Connecting mothers to what matters most

The Penguin infant protection solution offers the possibility for family members to directly connect with their infant during their stay in open crib environments within the NICU.

Safety, Comfort, and convenience

Our tags are equipped with functionalities like tamper detection and exit detection, further enhancing the security measures. The ankle bracelet is compact and comfortable, ensuring no discomfort for the baby.

How?

By employing entirely safe and harmless BLE technology and deploying BLE anchors within the facility, we improve visibility while seamlessly integrating with existing systems. The infant tags can precisely identify locations indoors, accurate to within a sub-meter range, enabling us to trigger alerts in the event of unauthorized movements. Additionally, the application is equipped with sensors that monitor the infant's condition, ensuring alerts are sent if any tampering or tearing is detected.

Learn more Request a Demo

Why BLE 5.1?

Accuracy is recognized as the primary distinguishing factor among various technologies, and BLE 5.1 unquestionably stands at the top in terms of precision. It offers sub-meter accuracy while consuming minimal power, incurring lower costs, and imposing no additional burden on IT infrastructure. Furthermore, its exceptional compatibility extends to all output devices, including mobile phones.

Related article: BLE 5.1 vs UWB

How it works?

Thanks to BLE 5.1, tags emit CTE signals, which are subsequently captured by the anchors. These signals are then sampled into IQ values, which are reported to the positioning engine. PenguinIN's cutting-edge algorithms are then employed to precisely determine the location of objects.

Harnessing AI for safety and security.

In various sectors, including healthcare and manufacturing, Penguin's PenSafe utilizes location technology applications to aid a diverse range of operations. Our dedication is to consistently introduce enhanced safety solutions for real-world challenges.
